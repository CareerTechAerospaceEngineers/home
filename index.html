Why This Matters:

We created this group

to raise awareness and gather support to help combat the destruction caused by species that have been introduced to a habitat they dont belong in

Usually invasive species are introduced by people who either accidentally release animals or flora into the surrounding area or by somebody who released them without knowing the effect they would have

According to NNSS, Invasive Species causes a 5% global loss in the economy and resulted in more than 40% of the extinction of species in the last 25 years

Alaska is a big place, about 365,000,000 acres, with a coast line longer than the other 49 states combined. Alaska is a major location for: sea, air shipping, tourism, and production. A large amount of imported goods give invasive species alot of ways in. If we do not control all these invasive species in the future our state will just be filled with them and they will impact our daily life greatly. Invasive species could bring unwanted pathogens or a plant that chokes out all the others. We need do do something about it and we need to do it now.

Policies and Events:

As a group there are some policies we want in place

For one we want a ban in place that prevents the release of any un-native species to any givin area. We also believe you should have to have a permit to stock any type of organism in its givin habitat

Right now we are going around are local hometown and gathering groups to help us find crawfish in Kodiacs waters

Goals:

Here are the immediate goals of Invasive Species of Alaska

  • Bring This to the attention of Alaska
  • Require a Permit to stock
  • Contain already invasive species by isolating the area
  • Encourage the extermination and or hunting of said species by making the local public aware
  • Pressure Alaska Fish + Game to help us by creating petitions to get regulations in place

Finances:

We are a non-profit organization

We make money by donations of people sympathetic to our cause and those who care for their future in Alaska. We often hold fun runs and set up booths at local events. We have also invested in merchandise that people passionate about the stop of invasive species can purchase. All of our funds go towards advertisement and back into the organization to continue to put on events and host at meets such as the Alaska State Fair. A large portion of the recieved funds go to charity, we donate alot of it to colleges that are willing to equip there students to go out and actually stop invasive species either by capturing or eradicating said species.
